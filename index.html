About Me

Background & Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry at GITAM University, Visakhapatnam, India. My research is rooted in computational and theoretical chemistry, with a focus on understanding complex molecular phenomena in soft matter, biological systems, and novel solvents.

Before joining GITAM, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto (2016–2023) under Prof. Hue Sun Chan, where I developed coarse-grained and analytical models for liquid-liquid phase separation in membraneless organelles — one of the most exciting frontiers in biophysics.

I completed my Ph.D. at S. N. Bose National Centre for Basic Sciences (Jadavpur University) in 2016, investigating heterogeneity and chemical dynamics in molten multi-component systems, including deep eutectic solvents and ionic liquids.

I am a recipient of the prestigious Ramanujan Fellowship (SERB, 2023) and am passionate about mentoring graduate and undergraduate students in computational research.

Research

Interests & Publications

🧬

Liquid-Liquid Phase Separation in Membraneless Organelles

Developing sequence-dependent coarse-grained models to understand how intrinsically disordered proteins (IDPs) undergo phase separation — relevant to gene regulation and disease.

💧

Deep Eutectic Solvents & Ionic Liquids

Using molecular dynamics simulations to probe microstructure, hydrogen-bond dynamics, dielectric relaxation, and transport properties in DESs and ILs.

⚛️

Intrinsically Disordered Proteins

Investigating hydration dynamics, conformational behavior, and binding of IDPs using theory and simulation — exploring how sequence encodes function.

🖥️

Quantum Computing

Exploring applications of quantum algorithms to chemical simulation problems, an emerging research interest at the boundary of chemistry and computer science.

🔬

Protein Stability in Non-Aqueous Solvents

Understanding how non-aqueous environments alter protein structure, stability and function, with implications for green chemistry and industrial biotechnology.
